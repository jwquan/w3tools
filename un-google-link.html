Un-Google Link

Best Free Online Un-Google link tool

Works with both Google search results and Adsense results. Useful if you want to know if your Adsense campaign points to the correct URL or you don't want the Google search engine to know that you actually clicked a link.


More information and examples for each parameter

Campaign Source

utm_source

Required.

Use utm_sourceto identify a search engine, newsletter name, or other source.

Example: google

Campaign Medium

utm_medium

Required.

Use utm_mediumto identify a medium such as email or cost-per- click.

Example: cpc

Campaign Name

utm_campaign

Required.

Used for keyword analysis. Use utm_campaignto identify a specific product promotion or strategic campaign.

Example: utm_campaign=spring_sale

Campaign Term

utm_term

Used for paid search. Use utm_termto note the keywords for this ad.

Example: running+shoes

Campaign Content

utm_content

Used for A/B testing and content-targeted ads. Use utm_contentto differentiate ads or links that point to the same URL.

Examples: logolink or textlink
